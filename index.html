Our Approach

Momentum’s approach is focused on building intuition through shifts in perspective, rather than simply “rote” learning. As high school students, we can relate to fellow NYC students, allowing us to build a deeper connection that resembles a friend more than a teacher. The tutors themselves are a tight-knit family who learn and grow together every day. While your child will only be interacting with one tutor at a time, multiple tutors from Momentum will help plan the tutoring strategy for your child. You or your child can request your tutor’s phone number for help outside of tutoring sessions.
